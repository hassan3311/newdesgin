Introduction

Welcome to IBB Petroleum (“IBB”, “we”, “our”, “us”). We value the trust you place in us when you visit our website, request services, or interact with our team. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website www.ibbpetroleum.com (the “Site”) or use our petroleum fueling services, customer support, and related communications.

By accessing our Site or engaging with our services, you agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our practices, please do not use our services. We are committed to compliance with applicable privacy laws including GDPR, CCPA, and Michigan data protection regulations.

Our commitment: Your information is used to deliver excellent fuel solutions, maintenance, and support — never sold to third parties for marketing.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you express an interest in our services, request a quote, contact support, or schedule maintenance. We may also automatically collect certain technical data.

Personal Data You Provide

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, company name, job title.
  • Service Details: Information about your fueling station, equipment type, maintenance history, project requirements, and location address (e.g., station address).
  • Communications: Messages, emails, chat transcripts, feedback, or call recordings (with consent where required).
  • Billing Information: When you engage our paid services, we collect billing address, tax ID, and payment details via secure third-party processors (we do not store full credit card numbers).

Automatically Collected Data

  • Device & Usage Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, pages visited, date/time stamps, and clickstream data.
  • Cookies & Similar Technologies: We use essential and analytical cookies to improve site performance and user experience (see section on Cookies).
  • Location Data: General geographic location based on IP address (city/region) to tailor service offerings.

How We Use Your Information

IBB Petroleum uses your information for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Delivering Services: To process service requests, schedule maintenance, dispatch emergency support, and complete fueling equipment projects.
  • Communication: To respond to inquiries, provide quotes, send service updates, invoices, and important notices.
  • Improvement & Analytics: To analyze website usage, enhance user experience, and develop new petroleum solutions.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with regulatory obligations, prevent fraud, and enforce our terms.
  • Marketing (Opt-out available): To send you newsletters, promotional offers, and industry insights only if you have consented or requested such communications. You may unsubscribe anytime.

We never sell your personal information to third parties for their own marketing purposes.

Sharing & Disclosure

We may share your information in limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties who assist with payment processing, IT infrastructure, customer support, and analytics (contractually obligated to protect your data).
  • Business Transfers: In the event of merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred with notice.
  • Legal Obligations: If required by law, subpoena, or governmental request to protect rights, safety, or investigate fraud.
  • With Your Consent: We may share information for other purposes with your explicit direction.
Partner ecosystem: We collaborate with brands like BP, Exxon, Emco, and PEI to deliver fueling equipment. However, these partners do not receive your personal data unless directly needed for warranty or service fulfillment.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking to enhance functionality and understand visitor preferences. Cookies are small text files stored on your device.

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for site navigation, security, and form submissions.
  • Analytics Cookies: We use Google Analytics and internal metrics to improve content. These collect anonymized data about page visits, traffic sources.
  • Preferences: To remember your language and region settings.

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect website performance. By continuing to use our site, you consent to our use of cookies as described.

Data Security & Retention

IBB Petroleum implements industry-standard security measures including encryption (SSL/TLS), firewalls, access controls, and regular security audits. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure; we encourage you to protect your credentials.

Retention: We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, comply with legal obligations (e.g., tax records, safety compliance), resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Typically, service records are retained for 7 years.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable privacy laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.). These rights include:

  • Access: Request a copy of personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request erasure of your data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Opt-out of Marketing: Unsubscribe from promotional emails via the link in our communications or by contacting us.
  • Data Portability: Receive a machine-readable copy of your data.
  • Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your information in certain circumstances.

To exercise your rights, please contact our Privacy Officer using the details below. We will respond within 30 days. No fee will be charged unless the request is excessive or repetitive.

Children's Privacy

Our services are directed to businesses, commercial entities, and adults over the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us immediately, and we will delete such information.
