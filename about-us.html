Our Story

IBB Petroleum Services Inc. has been a cornerstone in the petroleum equipment industry, dedicated to the distribution, supply, and maintenance of industrial commercial fueling systems. With over 18 years of experience, we have grown into a full-service partner for gas stations, convenience stores, and commercial fleet operators.

We take pride in our track record: 874+ happy clients, 398 completed projects, and currently 42 projects under execution. Every project reflects our commitment to safety, innovation, and operational excellence.

Our team of experts provides you with comprehensive and specialized guidance, leading you step by step towards optimal solutions and decisions. Whether it's a new build, retrofit, or emergency maintenance, IBB Petroleum is your ideal petroleum partner.
